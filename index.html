The Lazza Story..


The Company was founded in 1972 by Mr. M C John a retired senior
bureaucrat in the Kerala government. It is currently led by Mr. Simon John and
his team of eminent directors. The company is a pioneer in the ice cream
industry in South India. The first factory was started in Kochi and today has
over 10 factories and other ancilliary units spread across South India. The 10
factories put together has a production capacity that is among the top 5 in the
country. The company also has brand experience centres in Ernakulam,
Alapuzha, Kottayam and Trissur districts of and Mangalore district of
KarnatakaThe company overseas operations in parts of Middle East and Africa.
